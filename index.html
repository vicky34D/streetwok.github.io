OUR STORY

OUR MOTO

Our motto is to build a strong and welcoming bikers community — a place where riders can connect, share their journeys, support each other, and feel at home. At StreetWok Bikers Café, every visit is more than just food; it's about creating bonds, celebrating the passion for riding, and growing together as a united biking family.

ABOUT OUR CAFE

Located in the heart of Mukundapur, StreetWok is more than a pitstop. It's a sanctuary for those who live on two wheels. We serve bold flavors, hot coffee, and the best stories from the road. Whether you ride a superbike or a scooter, you belong here.

OUR JOURNEY

Started in 2024 with a single wok and a dream, we've grown into the city's favorite hangout spot. From weekend breakfast rides to late-night chai runs, we've been part of thousands of kilometers of memories.
